About

The modern world is busy.
It leaves little time and space for reflection, curiosity and conversation.
Ikiwai creates space for meaningful conversation.

How is works

  • Bring together 4-5 friends and sign up for Ikiwai
  • Recieve a weekly question, piece of writing or poetry to reflect on
  • Start a meaningful conversation within your group - Sunday brunch, skype or email group
  • Send us your group reflections and contribute to the Ikiwai community
